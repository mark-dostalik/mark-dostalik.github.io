Random maze

In 2023, I developed a high-resolution random maze generator for the visual artist Jan Dotřel. Two standard approaches were used – Prim's algorithm and recursive backtracking. Python code implementing these random maze generation algorithms is available on my GitHub.

The maze generation animation was used in the video documentary Geometria Sacra - The Labyrinths of Luca Pacioli that accompanied an art exhibition of the same name that took place in the fall of 2023 at the Italian Gallery of Villa Cernigliaro.
